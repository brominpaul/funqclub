Funq Account: A Student's first ever bank account.

  

Why do we need a Funq Bank Account?


Money is life skill to be picked up early.

It is time to re-imagine Student Banking in India. That boring savings account for minors has to go.

The kid's first bank account for pocket-money:

  • zero balance account
  • safe & secure like any bank account
  • sleek & fun app + card
  • not having to connect to parents own account or share their card
  • with full parental control (not needed for the 18+)
